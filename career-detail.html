Group Financial Controller

Job Highlights

  • Professional Advancement: Annual performance evaluations with opportunities for promotion. Potential to rise to a chief-level position.
  • Competitive Compensation: Attractive salary package with performance-based incentives.
  • Equity Participation: Eligibility for the Employee Share Option Scheme (ESOS).
  • Company Growth: The company is on a path towards an IPO and expanding internationally.

Job Description

We are seeking an experienced and detail-oriented Financial Controller to join our dynamic team. This role requires a blend of technical expertise in finance and accounting, strong leadership abilities, and excellent communication skills. The successful candidate will play a pivotal role in shaping our financial strategy and ensuring effective financial management across the company.


Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct professional financial analysis, focusing on profit and loss (P&L) assessments.
  • Develop and implement financial plan, aligning with the company's strategic objectives.
  • Manage cash flows effectively, ensuring optimal liquidity for business operations.
  • Utilize expertise in tax planning to minimize liabilities and enhance financial efficiency.
  • Liaise with auditors and manage tax-related risks, ensuring compliance with regulations.
  • Analyze and report on financial risks and develop mitigation strategies.
  • Initiate and oversee cost reduction strategies to optimize financial resources.
  • Manage and optimize banking relationships and loan facilities.
  • Lead, train, and coach the finance team, enhancing their skills and performance.
  • Oversee the recruitment process for finance team members, identifying key skills and attributes.
  • Assist on the collection of payments.
  • Actively participate in strategic decisions, offering insights based on financial analysis.
  • Collaborate in mergers and acquisitions (M&A), including company acquisitions and share allocation strategies.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in finance, Accounting, or related field; MBA or professional certification (e.g., CPA, CMA) preferred.
  • experience as a finance managerial & controller or similar role for at least 8 years.
  • Able to communicate in Mandarin is a bonus.
  • Strong skills in financial analysis, planning, and forecasting.
  • Expertise in tax planning and risk management.
  • Excellent leadership and team management abilities.
  • Exceptional communication and presentation skills.
