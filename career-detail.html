Senior Financial Advisor

Job Description

We are seeking a Senior Financial Advisor to join our dynamic team at Fintos. The ideal candidate will be responsible for providing expert financial advice, developing personalized financial plans for clients, and guiding them through their financial journey. As a Senior Financial Advisor, you will play a key role in helping clients achieve their financial goals while fostering a culture of financial literacy and empowerment.


Responsibilities

  • Meet with clients to discuss their financial goals and assess their financial situation.
  • Develop and implement customized financial plans based on clients needs
  • Provide strategic advice on investment opportunities and manage clients' investment portfolios.

Job Requirements

  • Education: Bachelor's degree in Finance, Economics, Business Administration, or a related field. CFP (Certified Financial Planner) or other relevant certifications preferred.
  • Experience: Minimum of 8 years of experience in financial planning or advisory roles
  • Analytical Skills: Strong ability to analyze financial data and develop strategic plans.

Skills

  • Financial Analysis
  • Risk Management
