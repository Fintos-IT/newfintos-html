Group Operation Manager

Job Highlights

  • Professional Advancement: Annual performance evaluations with opportunities for promotion. Potential to rise to a chief-level position.
  • Competitive Compensation: Attractive salary package with performance-based incentives.
  • Equity Participation: Eligibility for the Employee Share Option Scheme (ESOS).
  • Company Growth: The company is on a path towards an IPO and expanding internationally.

Job Description

We are seeking an experienced and detail-oriented Group Operation Manager to join our dynamic team. This role requires a blend of technical expertise in finance and accounting, strong leadership abilities, and excellent communication skills. The successful candidate will play a pivotal role in shaping our financial strategy and ensuring effective financial management across the company.


Key Responsibilities

  • Oversee daily operations across various departments, ensuring smooth and efficient processes.
  • Ensure the smooth execution of the sales process and overall business operations.
  • Monitor and optimize the entire operational workflow to achieve maximum efficiency and productivity.
  • Develop and implement operational policies, procedures, and best practices to optimize performance and ensure compliance with relevant regulations.
  • Continuously update and refine SOPs to align with industry standards and organizational needs.
  • Collaborate with senior management to develop and execute long-term strategic plans and initiatives.
  • Drive the adoption of technology and digital solutions to enhance operational efficiency and support business objectives.
  • Analyze KPIs and financial data to identify trends, areas for improvement, and opportunities for growth.
  • Prepare and submit regular sales and OKR performance reports to top management, highlighting key achievements and areas for improvement.
  • Provide insights and recommendations for enhancing the company's sales performance and overall growth.
  • Maintain strong relationships with industry stakeholders, including clients, partners, and regulators, to expand business opportunities and ensure client satisfaction.
  • Ensure all operations adhere to applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards.
  • Stay well-versed with legal and regulatory changes to ensure ongoing compliance.
  • Collaborate with other departments, such as sales, marketing, and finance, to achieve organizational goals and support overall business growth.
  • Monitor the operation team's efforts to keep the company's systems updated and aligned with industry best practices.
  • Develop and maintain a high-performing customer service team.
  • Implement performance management strategies to ensure the customer service team consistently meets and exceeds expectations.
  • Provide regular feedback, coaching, and training to improve team members' skills and foster a culture of continuous improvement.

Qualifications

  • At least 5 years of experience in an operational managerial position within a bank.
  • Proven experience in drafting and implementing SOPs.
  • Bachelor’s degree in business administration; MBA preferred.
  • Familiarity with laws, regulations, and compliance standards in the banking industry.
  • Strong analytical skills with the ability to interpret complex data and provide actionable insights.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to speak Mandarin is essential due to the business nature.
  • Proficiency in relevant digital tools and technology to support operational efficiency.
